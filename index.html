Rules Lockdown

Goal: remove ambiguity before touching final art or print layouts.

Core Win Condition

Lock the exact victory target and whether coal must be deposited or merely carried.

  • Is the coal requirement always 12, or does it scale by player count?
  • Does coal in inventory count, or must coal be deposited into the furnace?
  • If deposited coal counts, is depositing one coal always one action?
  • Do face-down coal cards count toward victory?

Designer Answer

Air Timer And Difficulty

Define the standard starting timer and how difficulty scales.

  • What is the default air value for 1, 2, 3, and 4 players?
  • Does burning coal always deplete air by one round?
  • Should there be easy/standard/hard setup values?
  • Does any event restore or add air?

Designer Answer

Spiders

This is the largest unresolved rules area and should be decided before layout updates.

  • Are spiders stationary in the main game, or do they move at end of round?
  • If they move, do they use base-card edge numbers exactly as drafted?
  • When is a spider rotated versus aligned normally?
  • Does a spider cover and block the card beneath it?
  • What happens when water, repellent, elevator, or another spider occupies the nest?

Designer Answer

Pickaxe: Dig Deep Or Reroute

Current art and latest rules disagree, so this needs a named final behavior.

  • Is the final action called Dig Deep, Reroute, or something else?
  • Does it remove the top card, swap stacked cards, or reveal/reposition cards?
  • Can it target a space containing a spider, rope, repellent, pawn, or river?
  • Does it consume two Pickaxe cooldowns, remove cards from play, or both?

Designer Answer

Inventory System

Confirm the face-up/face-down cooldown loop and remove old discard-hand language.

  • Are all player inventory cards visible to the table?
  • When all cards are face down, do they refresh immediately or at turn end?
  • Can a player choose Stow Item for either or both required actions?
  • When gained, do all item cards enter face up?

Designer Answer

Turn Structure

Clarify what is mandatory and what can be skipped.

  • Must each player use exactly 1 move and exactly 2 actions?
  • Is "move nowhere" a legal move?
  • Can rescuing another pawn from an adjacent space always be done as the move?
  • Can a player act from the final progress circle before moving away?

Designer Answer

Rivers And Flooding

Confirm the exact flood procedure and how river movement resolves.

  • Does flooding chain until no more spaces can flood, or only one wave per round?
  • Are base cards with rotated cards on them considered empty for flooding?
  • Can a rope-covered river stop forced river movement?
  • If a pawn is trapped by river movement, what exact penalty and placement apply?

Designer Answer

Rope And Repellent

These cards create board infrastructure, so retrieval and coverage rules need one clean policy.

  • Do Rope and Repellent stay on the board as rotated cards?
  • Does taking them back require an action and a move, or only one of those?
  • Can they be washed away by newly revealed water?
  • Can Rope or Repellent be placed if the card underneath is unrevealed?

Designer Answer

Setup And Starting Positions

Make setup teachable and eliminate bracketed notes from the rules.

  • Can multiple players start on the same space?
  • Are starts limited to corners, any edge, or designer choice?
  • Is the elevator always hidden in the center 3x3 under another card?
  • Should the coal/high-rubble random-removal step stay exactly as written?

Designer Answer

Component Counts

Card counts must match the locked rules and available art.

  • Final mine deck count by card type?
  • Final starting item count by player count?
  • Final tool chest mix by player count?
  • How many rule/reference cards should be included?
